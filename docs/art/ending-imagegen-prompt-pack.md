# Ending Image Generation Prompt Pack

Date: 2026-04-20

These prompts are for missing MVP ending images. Use the local imagegen CLI after `OPENAI_API_KEY` is valid.

Common constraints for every prompt:

- SBTI testing inspired low-saturation geometric cutout illustration.
- Visible low-poly faceted paper planes.
- Flat 2D personality-test result card feeling.
- Vertical `1024x1536`.
- Large negative space for UI text overlays.
- No text, labels, logo, watermark, or UI words.
- Avoid photorealism, glossy 3D, painterly fantasy art, anime, chibi, Disney, Popmart, high saturation, busy backgrounds, and heavy outlines.

### `endings/corruption-flood.png`

Corruption flood ending. An overflowing treasury with faceted coins and ledgers spilling like a quiet flood. Tiny officials stand nervously around a money chest. Wealth feels excessive, comic, and dangerous.

### `endings/frontier-collapse.png`

Frontier collapse ending. A quiet ancient border outpost after defeat: broken banner, empty watchtower, distant faceted mountains. No bodies, no gore. Tragic but understated, with a large pale sky.

### `endings/military-takeover.png`

Military takeover ending. A stern faceted general calmly stands before an empty imperial chair. A small crown lies on the floor. Simple banners and spears, absurdly polite coup mood, no blood.

Bureaucratic suffocation ending. An emperor is almost hidden behind towering stacks of faceted memorial scrolls and seals. Tiny officials surround him. The system looks perfect but airless and absurd.

### `endings/alchemy-death.png`

Alchemy death ending. A tiny ancient alchemy room with faceted furnace, smoke, spilled golden pill, emperor silhouette collapsed comically behind a low table. No gore; dark humor but tasteful.

### `endings/old-age-succession.png`

Old-age succession ending. A very old emperor's small crown and seal are being gently passed to a new empty throne, with tiny historians changing scrolls in the background. Peaceful, funny, slightly absurd, no deathbed realism, no gore.
